Professional Biography

Learn about the education, career, and achievements of Zoi Lama

Zoi Lama

Zoi Lama is a licensed attorney in Greece with over 15 years of experience practicing law. She specializes in property law, family law, and inheritance law, with particular expertise in assisting foreign investors purchasing property in Crete.

Based in Heraklion, Crete, she serves both local residents and international clients, providing personalized legal solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

With a commitment to excellence and integrity, Zoi has built a reputation for providing clear, effective legal counsel in complex matters while maintaining a client-centered approach.
