About Zoi Lama

Dedicated to providing exceptional legal services with integrity and professionalism

Professional Excellence

As a licensed attorney in Greece with over 15 years of experience, I have dedicated my career to helping clients navigate complex legal matters with confidence and clarity.

During my studies, I attended a law course at Rotterdam Erasmus University, where I was taught international law subjects such as European Environmental Law and International Sea Law. In 1993, I began my eighteen-month training in order to qualify as a lawyer, while at the same time I was recommended and employed as a bank clerk in The CB of NS, Heraklion, gaining valuable financial and banking experience and commencing my legal career.


In 1995, I succeeded in the examinations before the Appeal Court of Crete and was qualified to be enrolled in the Heraklion Law Bar, where I have been a member ever since. For more than twenty years, I have worked as an Independent Lawyer, handling cases in Greek civil and criminal courts and representing both European and non-European clients in property transactions.


My associates, who include lawyers, engineers, accountants, and notaries, are highly qualified, fluent in English, and experienced in property surveys, working under my close supervision and instructions.


For over fifteen years, my law firm has also been included in the list of lawyers recommended by the British Consulate to its citizens regarding criminal or civil cases in Crete, and I have successfully handled numerous serious first-degree criminal offences, both at the questioning stage and before the competent Court.


Based in Heraklion, Crete, I serve both local residents and international clients, providing personalized legal solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.
